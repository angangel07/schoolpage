School Rules

  1. Uniform is mandatory

    All students must wear the official school uniform at all times. This helps promote equality and a sense of community.

  2. 85% attendance required

    Attendance is essential for academic success. Students must maintain a minimum of 85% attendance to sit for examinations.

  3. Discipline must be maintained

    Respect and discipline are expected at all times. Disruptive behavior will be addressed promptly.

  4. No mobile phones during classes

    To maintain focus during lessons, mobile phones must be switched off or kept away.

  5. Respect towards teachers and peers

    Mutual respect is key to a positive learning environment. Bullying or harassment in any form is strictly prohibited.

  1. No food in the library

    To preserve the resources and maintain cleanliness, food and drinks are not allowed in the library.

  2. Respect school property

    Students are expected to handle school property responsibly and report any damages immediately.

  3. No bullying or harassment

    A safe environment is our priority. Any form of bullying or harassment will be dealt with strictly.
